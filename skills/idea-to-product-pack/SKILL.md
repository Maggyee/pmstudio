# Idea-to-Product Pack

Use this workflow when the user provides a raw product idea and wants a complete product plan.

## Inputs

- Product idea.
- Target market or user segment if provided.
- Constraints such as launch date, platform, business model, or industry.

## Output Sections

1. Product positioning.
2. Target users.
3. User pain points.
4. Value proposition.
5. PRD draft.
6. Core feature list.
7. User flow.
8. Prototype page structure.
9. Market opportunity.
10. Competitor analysis direction.
11. MVP scope.
12. Product roadmap.
13. Executive summary.

## Process

1. Normalize the idea into a concise product thesis.
2. Identify the target user, buyer, and evaluator when they differ.
3. Convert pain points into outcome-oriented requirements.
4. Draft PRD sections with objective, scope, core flows, and success metrics.
5. Map requirements into prototype screens and page hierarchy.
6. Summarize opportunity, competitive angle, and MVP path.

## Quality Bar

- Be specific enough for a team review.
- Keep assumptions visible.
- Avoid generic AI-product claims.
- Prefer launchable MVP scope over broad platform promises.
